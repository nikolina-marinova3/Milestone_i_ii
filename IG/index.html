Services

Landscape design

Boost your spirit by creating a unique landscape design of your yard. Infra Ltd experts can help you design and build the best landscaping and surfacing project. We can also provide complete or partial redesign of your garden and/or the surrounding landscape to meet your individual needs and budget requirements.

Driveways

Install your new stunning driveway or restore existing one to the highest standards with our dedicated specialists. Infra Ltd take pride in our expertise when it comes to driveways. Whether you decide on block paving, resins, or tarmac driveway, we will give you advice about lighting, steps, and entrances to complete the look.

Garden and Patio

Expand your outdoor living space to enjoy with family and friends by building a larger and luxurious patio. Infra Ltd specialists can assist you with your design and material choice. We have access to a vast selection of products to choose from including block paving, natural stone, sandstone, brick paving, concrete slabs and permeable paving available in different colours and finishes.

Surfacing

Make your property stand out from the rest with our expertly installed tarmac, block paving, and resins. To ensure the longevity of your driveway, our team at Infra Ltd only use quality materials.
